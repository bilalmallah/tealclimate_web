TEAL: Technology, Environment, Agriculture, Landscapes

TEAL Climate combines indigenous wisdom and cutting-edge technology to protect our environment.

Integrating Tradition & Technology

TEAL Climate on a mission to give life to nature-based solutions through a fusion of indigenous knowledge, cutting-edge technology, mathematical modelling, remote sensing, and artificial intelligence.

TEAL Climate provides tech solutions for climate preservation and helps organizations achieve Net Zero. The service includes research and development, greenhouse gas accounting and management, biodiversity impact assessment and water management.

Meet Our Team

The TEAL Climate team consists of researchers and experts who have mastered the science and art of turning research into impactful products for the good of society.
